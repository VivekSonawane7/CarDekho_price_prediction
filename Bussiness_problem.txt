PROJECT TITLE: Used Car Price Prediction – Machine Learning Solution


BUSINESS CONTEXT:
Online automobile resale platforms such as CarDekho host thousands of used car listings.
Pricing vehicles accurately is a major challenge due to multiple influencing factors such as
brand value, vehicle age, engine capacity, power, mileage, fuel type, and market demand.

Incorrect pricing can lead to:

• Delayed sales due to overpriced listings
• Revenue loss due to underpricing
• Reduced customer trust
• Poor buyer-seller matching efficiency


BUSINESS PROBLEM:
How can we build a data-driven pricing model that accurately predicts the
resale value of a used car based on its specifications and historical listing data?


OBJECTIVE:
Develop a machine learning regression model that predicts the selling price
of used cars using structured vehicle attributes.

The model should:

• Capture non-linear relationships between features
• Minimize prediction error
• Generalize well to unseen car listings
• Be deployable in a real-world environment


SUCCESS METRICS:

• High R² Score on test dataset
• Low Mean Absolute Error (MAE)
• Low Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)
• Minimal overfitting (small train-test performance gap)


BUSINESS VALUE:

The deployed model can:

• Assist sellers in setting competitive prices
• Help buyers evaluate fair market value
• Improve platform pricing transparency
• Increase transaction efficiency
• Enhance user trust and engagement


SOLUTION APPROACH:

1. Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
2. Correlation Analysis
3. Feature Engineering
4. Hybrid Encoding Strategy (Target Encoding + One-Hot Encoding)
5. Model Training & Comparison
6. Final Model Selection (Random Forest Regressor)
7. Deployment using Streamlit


FINAL OUTCOME:

A production-ready machine learning pipeline capable of predicting
used car resale prices with strong generalization performance
and real-time deployment capability.
